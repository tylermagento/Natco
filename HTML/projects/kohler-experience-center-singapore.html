KOHLER EXPERIENCE CENTER

Singapore

An interactive showcase for Kohler’s bathroom and spa products, the Experience Center tells a story about each collection through five semi-open display suites, each designed with unique themes – fashion, hi-tech, family, classic and black.

The “pop-up” construction of each display creates the stage for a quirky and innovative product showcase.

Hand-selected furniture items complement the 19th century American home aesthetic that alludes to Kohler’s beginnings, and the Center’s historic three-storey conservation shophouse setting.

“Features such as laser-cut batik panels and Peranakan-inspired flooring tiles serve as an ode to the architecture and give a sense of place.”
